Buoy Info

The buoy at the Mackinac Straits West buoy station (NDBC Station 45175) is a Total Integrated Data Acquisition Systems (TIDAS) model S2 Yachts-900 marine research station equipped with an Acoustic Doppler Current Profiler. The buoy is removed each year during the winter months. As currently configured, the station collects wind direction, wind speed and gust speed, air and water surface temperatures, relative humidity, dew point, barometric pressure, solar radiation, significant wave height, dominant period of waves, wave direction, buoy battery voltage, and the depth and eastward and northward water velocities for each of 28 vertical bins of the water column.
